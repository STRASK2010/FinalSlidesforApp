Emotional Engagement Study

Emotion determines intent, preference and future behavior

Stephen Trask, MT

Emotion influences 95% of consumer behavior

  • When rational and emotional thoughts are at odds, emotion wins
  • Emotion drives preference and memory
  • Emotionally charged events and products are remembered better
  • Positive memories contain more contextual details

Emotions cause physiologic changes

  • The change in background probably suprised you, which can cause many of the following...

    1. Expressions emerge on a face
2. Heart rate increases, pupils dilate
3. Skin conductance changes and muscles contract
4. Most changes happen without conscious awareness

Quantifying these changes provides insight

  • These biometrics are surrogate markers for engagement
  • The process finds correlations
  • The output is a prediction of future behaviors
  • Clients can make better decisions to impact ROI
